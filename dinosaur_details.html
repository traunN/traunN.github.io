Experience Details

Step into a prehistoric world where dinosaurs roam free! This interactive experience, "Au milieu des dinosaures," developed with Unreal Engine 4 by Tumulte, offers a captivating 10-minute journey perfect for the whole family.

As a 3D Visual Developer, I was responsible for bringing these ancient creatures to life. This involved developing simple AI behaviors to give the dinosaurs lifelike movements and interactions, significantly enhancing the immersive quality of the experience.

A key part of my role also involved optimizing performance to ensure a smooth and seamless experience within a 360-degree 3D setup. This was crucial for maintaining immersion and preventing any technical glitches.

The project was a collaborative effort with a dedicated team, including:

  • 1x Production Manager
  • 4x Artists
  • 2x Programmers

This experience allows you to interact with dinosaurs at your will, making it a unique and unforgettable adventure for all ages.
